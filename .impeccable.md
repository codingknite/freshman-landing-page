## Design Context

### Users
Students aged 14–30 (high school through early-career professional) evaluating Freshman as an AI-powered study and exam prep tool. They are time-pressured, anxious about upcoming exams, and need to quickly understand value on first visit.

### Brand Personality
Bold, modern, energetic. Three words: **sharp, driven, confident**. Freshman should feel like a serious performance tool, not a playful study toy.

### Emotional Goal
Visitors should feel immediate confidence and urgency to start studying smarter, with clear trust signals and low friction to begin.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Theme**: Light mode primary.
- **Visual tone**: Refined minimalism with bold typographic hierarchy, crisp surfaces, and navy/indigo-tinted neutrals.
- **Anti-references**:
  - No gamified mascot vibes
  - No sterile enterprise gray layouts
  - No generic AI gradients/glow effects
  - No empty "build your own" canvas feel

### Design Principles
1. **Message first** - the value proposition must be readable in seconds.
2. **Bold but controlled** - emphasis comes from typography and hierarchy, not visual noise.
3. **Trust through clarity** - clean spacing, predictable navigation, and clear actions.
4. **Mobile-first conversion** - hero copy and CTA stay legible and actionable on small screens.
5. **Every element earns its place** - no decorative patterns that do not strengthen comprehension.
